01 — Animal Scanner

Every creature,
identified.

Point your phone at any animal — on land, in water, or in the air — and Burd's Animal Scanner identifies it in real time. Beyond birds, Animal Scanner extends Burd's signature identification engine across the entire animal kingdom.

  • Photo ID

    Point your camera at any animal for instant identification.

  • Sound ID

    Identify species by their calls, songs, and ambient sounds.

  • Real-time detection

    Live bounding boxes and confidence scores as you move.

  • 10,000+ species

    Mammals, reptiles, insects, amphibians, fish, and more.

Coming Soon iOS & Android

02 — Habitat Scanner

Read the
landscape.

Wildlife leaves traces long before it appears. Habitat Scanner lets you scan the environment itself — trees, nests, eggs, burrows — to understand which species live there, breed there, and pass through.

  • Tree & plant ID

    Identify any tree, shrub, or plant from bark, leaf, or silhouette.

  • Nest identification

    Determine which species built a nest by its structure and materials.

  • Egg recognition

    Identify eggs by size, colour, and speckle pattern.

  • Burrows & shelters

    Recognize animal dens, burrows, and shelter structures.
