Built for birders, not algorithms

Burd is where your sightings become part of a local story — a feed of real birds from real places, a journal of what you've found, and a field guide that grows with every trip. Whether you're chasing a rarity or noting the robin on your fence, Burd keeps it simple, social, and rooted in the field.
